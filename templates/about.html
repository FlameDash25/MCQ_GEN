About MCQ Generator

The MCQ Generator is a powerful tool designed to help educators and students create multiple-choice questions (MCQs) from uploaded documents. Whether you're preparing for exams or creating quizzes, this tool simplifies the process by automatically generating questions based on the content you provide.

Our team is passionate about education and technology, and we strive to make learning more accessible and efficient. With features like PDF, TXT, and DOCX support, the MCQ Generator is your go-to solution for creating high-quality MCQs in minutes.
